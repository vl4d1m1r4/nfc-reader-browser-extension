How It Helps You

Plug & Play Readers

Connect your external USB Smart Card Reader. The extension automatically lists available devices so you can select the one you want to use in seconds.

Tap to Type

Don't type long ID numbers manually. Just click a text box on any website, tap your card on the reader, and we'll type the UID for you instantly.

Your Data, Your Format

Need the ID with colons? Dashes? Or just raw numbers? Configure the output format easily: AA BB CC , AA:BB:CC, AA-BB-CC, or AABBCC.
