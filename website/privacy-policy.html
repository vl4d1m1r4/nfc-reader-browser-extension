Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how the NFC Reader extension handles your data.

Data Collection and Usage

Our philosophy is simple: your data belongs to you.

  • No Data Storage: The extension does not store, log, or archive any personal information or usage data from the user.
  • UID Reading Only: The extension is designed to strictly read the Unique Identifier (UID) from the connected smart card.
  • No Data Access: The extension does not access, read, or look into the encrypted or unencrypted data stored on the smart card itself. It only interacts with the card's serial number/UID.
  • No External Transmission: The UID read from the card is only used to populate the active field in your browser as requested. It is not sent to any external servers by the extension.
